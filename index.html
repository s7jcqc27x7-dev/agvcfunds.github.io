Our Thesis

Three forces converging — right now.

Asia is aging faster than any other region. The silver economy is on a trajectory toward $4.2 trillion by 2035 — yet the infrastructure to bring longevity science from lab to market in Asia remains fragmented and underfunded.

AGVC is not an investment fund. We are an operator — a hands-on cross-border accelerator that provides the commercial scaffolding, regulatory navigation, manufacturing access, and strategic partnerships that allow breakthrough science to land and thrive in APAC.

We believe the convergence of aging demographics, AI-driven biology, and capital flowing into Asia is the defining commercial opportunity of this decade — and Hong Kong is the best positioned gateway to capture it.
