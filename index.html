Our Thesis

Three forces converging — right now.

Asia is aging faster than any other region on earth. The silver economy is projected to reach $22 trillion by 2030 — yet the infrastructure to bring longevity science from lab to market in Asia remains fragmented and underfunded.

AGVC exists to close that gap. We are not an investment fund. We are an operator — a hands-on accelerator that provides the commercial scaffolding, regulatory navigation, manufacturing access, and capital market pathways that allow breakthrough science to land and scale across APAC.

We believe the convergence of aging, AI × biology, and capital flowing into Asia is the defining opportunity of this decade.

What We Do

Four services. One platform.

We are not a fund. We do not commit capital in exchange for equity as a standard practice. We are your cross-border operating partner — building the commercial, regulatory, and manufacturing foundation companies need to scale in Asia.

Service 03

Capital Markets & Listing Advisory

We connect pipeline companies to licensed broker-dealers and capital markets advisors in Hong Kong, guide founders through the full IPO journey (Pre-IPO through post-listing), and structure SPAC/SPV vehicles for eligible companies.

What We Deliver
  • HKEx listing readiness assessment (Chapter 8, 18A, 18C) — full scoping and planning
  • Pre-IPO: financial preparation, key advisor appointments, timetable
  • IPO project management across workstreams — legal, accounting, regulatory
  • Post-IPO: public company reporting, systems, and market expectation management
  • Introductions to licensed broker-dealers for capital placement (3–5% fee-sharing)
  • Family office and institutional LP network access
  • SPAC Sponsor Group positioning for mature pipeline targets — Chapter 18B/18C eligible
  • SPV and co-investment structuring alongside anchor capital
