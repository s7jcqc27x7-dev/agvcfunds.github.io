01

Business Landing Acceleration

企业落地加速服务

We act as your in-market operator in Hong Kong and the Greater Bay Area — handling everything required to turn a foreign company into a functioning local entity with real commercial traction.

我们担任您在香港及大湾区的驻地运营伙伴——全权处理外资企业在当地设立实体、实现商业化落地所需的一切事务。

  • HK entity formation, BRN registration & company secretarial
  • Corporate structuring, organisation design & strategy planning
  • Accounting, tax planning & reporting systems
  • Funding support & grant facilitation (InnoHK, ITF, HKIDS, Cyberport)
  • HKSTP ecosystem, GBA research institutes, hospital groups & CRO introductions
  • Buyer, manufacturer & distributor matching across GBA
  • IP structuring, patent liaison & R&D co-development framing
  • Share/option plan design; investor process support; M&A facilitation
  • 香港实体注册、商业登记及公司秘书服务
  • 集团架构设计、组织规划及战略布局
  • 会计核算、税务筹划及财务报告体系搭建
  • 资金支持与资助统筹（InnoHK、ITF、HKIDS、数码港）
  • 对接香港科技园生态圈、大湾区研究院、医院集团及CRO
  • 大湾区买家、制造商及分销商匹配对接
  • 知识产权架构、专利协调及研发合作框架
  • 股权/期权激励设计、投资者流程支持及并购协助

02

Local CDMO & Manufacturing Partnering

本地CDMO及制造合作

For biotech, pharma, and MedTech founders with validated IP, we identify and broker access to CDMOs and manufacturing partners across Hong Kong, GBA, and Southeast Asia.

针对拥有经验证知识产权的生物科技、制药及医疗器械创始人，我们在香港、大湾区及东南亚识别并对接合适的CDMO及制造合作伙伴。

  • CDMO identification and matchmaking across HK, Guangdong & ASEAN
  • Manufacturing and supply agreement negotiation
  • IP out-licensing to regional pharma, MedTech & manufacturing partners
  • Asset licensing deal structuring and milestone architecture
  • GMP readiness, quality systems & regulatory filing support
  • R&D co-development with local academic and institute partners
  • 香港、广东及东盟CDMO识别与精准匹配
  • 制造及供应协议谈判
  • 知识产权向区域制药、医疗器械及制造伙伴的授权转让
  • 资产授权交易架构及里程碑体系设计
  • GMP合规准备、质量体系及法规申报支持
  • 与本地学术机构及研究院联合研发合作
