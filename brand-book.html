01

Brand Essence

We aren't chasing the fountain of youth. We're building the infrastructure for the industrialization of geroscience.

AGVC is the world's first AI-driven venture catalyst dedicated to the longevity economy. We are an operator, not a passive fund — a hands-on cross-border accelerator that lands breakthrough science in Asia and scales it to the world.

What we stand for

i

Operator, not spectator

We build and launch companies. We provide the commercial scaffolding, regulatory navigation, and manufacturing access that turn validated science into scaled ventures.

ii

Bridge between East and West

We connect global deep-tech innovation with Asian capital, manufacturing, and the world's largest aging market.

iii

Science first

Every decision passes a rigorous biological stress test. We back teams with secured or in-licensed IP and a defined commercial roadmap.

iv

Longevity is the opportunity of our time

The 1990s belonged to the Internet, the 2020s to SaaS. The 2030s belong to Longevity — and the customer base is every living human being.

02

The Mark

The AGVC wordmark is set bold in Montserrat (800 weight) with a signature vertical gold-to-silver-to-black gradient — champagne gold at the top, brushed silver through the middle, deep black at the base. The name derives from Ag (the chemical symbol for silver, embodying the silver economy) and VC (venture capital): AG + VC = an "A-G-less" society. The metallic top-to-bottom flow gives the mark a premium, dimensional feel while keeping every letter legible on both light and dark backgrounds.

AGVC
AGVC
Primary — on dark
Reversed — on light

Clear space & minimum size

Always maintain clear space around the mark equal to the height of the star. Never place the logo smaller than 24px tall on screen or 12mm in print.

AGVC

The dashed boundary marks the minimum clear space. Keep all competing elements — text, images, edges — outside it.

Wordmark tagline lockup

When space allows, the mark may be paired with the tagline "Age, but less." set in Noto Serif italic, separated by a thin vertical rule.

Typeography

Two typefaces carry the entire system. Noto Serif — used in italic — brings editorial elegance to headlines and pull quotes. Inter handles all body copy, labels, and interface text with clean neutrality.

Chinese typography

For Simplified Chinese, Noto Sans SC is used throughout — it pairs cleanly with both Latin faces and maintains legibility at all sizes. Chinese headlines drop the italic styling used in English.

05

Voice & Tone

AGVC speaks with the confidence of an institutional operator and the precision of a scientist. We are declarative, never salesy. We let substance carry the message.

We do
Lead with substance and specifics
Use active, declarative statements
Say "support" and "endorse," not "invest"
Speak as an operator and builder
Respect the reader's intelligence
We don't
Use hype or superlatives without proof
Add instructional filler ("click here", "scroll down")
Over-explain or pad with adjectives
Sound like a consumer tech startup
Make claims we can't stand behind

06

The Tagline

Age, but less.
增长年岁，而不衰老。

The tagline is a play on the AGVC name and the company's purpose: to lessen the burden of age. Always set in Noto Serif italic (English). Never paraphrase or translate loosely.

07

In Practice

Digital surfaces

Dark navy backgrounds with a subtle grid or stardust texture. Generous whitespace. Gold reserved for a single accent per view — a CTA, an emphasized word, an eyebrow line.

Presentations & documents

Black or deep-navy title slides, white content slides. Serif italic for section openers. Data and diagrams in the navy-and-gold palette. Always credit sources in faint steel at the footer.

Business cards & print

Deep navy stock with gold foil for the star and wordmark. Contact details in Inter. Reverse side may carry the tagline centered in gold italic.
