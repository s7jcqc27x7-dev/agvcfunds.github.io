Justin Tso
Founder & General Partner
创始人兼普通合伙人

Justin leads AGVC’s cross-border acceleration platform, focused on longevity and health-tech landing across APAC and US–Asia deal origination. A life scientist turned venture operator, his journey spans biotechnology at Columbia University, the Biotech IPO team of the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, SPAC and IPO advisory at MSQ Ventures, and life-science venture building at RTW Investments. As Director of Longevity Global China, he has been building the longevity community through engaging educational events, wellness programming, and a growing network of aging scientists, clinicians, and founders across Asia.

曹展立领导 AGVC 的跨境加速平台，专注于长寿与健康科技在亚太的落地及中美亚洲的项目对接。他是一位由生命科学家转型的创投运营者，历经哥伦比亚大学生物技术研究、香港交易所生物科技 IPO 团队、MSQ Ventures 的 SPAC 与 IPO 顾问工作，以及 RTW Investments 的生命科学创投孵化。作为 Longevity Global 中国区总监，他通过丰富的教育活动、健康项目，以及遍布亚洲的科学家、临床医生与创始人网络，持续建设长寿社群。
