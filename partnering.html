AGVC selectively endorses and supports early-stage companies across four longevity verticals — teams with secured or in-licensed IP and a defined commercial roadmap for Asia.

AGVC 在四大长寿核心领域中，甄选并支持拥有已确权或授权知识产权、以及清晰海外增值路线的早期企业。

Four Focus Verticals

四大核心赛道

Areas we support and endorse.

我们关注的长寿行业板块。

Pre-Seed to Seed stage. We focus on teams with secured or in-licensed IP and a defined commercial roadmap in one of the following four verticals.

种子前至种子轮阶段。我们专注于拥有已确权或授权知识产权、并在以下四大赛道之一具备明确商业路线图的团队。

🧬
LongBios — Next-Gen Pharma
长寿医药 / 新一代制药
Longevity Therapeutics
长寿疗法

Accelerating geroscience through repurposed drug pipelines and the application of aging clock models. We endorse companies translating biological aging mechanisms into validated therapeutic programs.

通过药物重用管线及老化时钟模型的应用，加速老化科学的发展。我们支持将生物衰老机制转化为经验证治疗方案的企业。

⌚
GeronTech & Lifestyle Hardwares
高龄科技与生活穿戴设备
Wearables & Monitoring
可穿戴与监测

Developing high-performance wearables and tools that seamlessly integrate biological monitoring into daily life. We support teams building hardware platforms that generate clinically meaningful aging data.

开发高性能的穿戴式设备和工具，将生物监测无缝融入日常生活。我们支持构建能生成具有临床意义老化数据硬件平台的团队。

🤖
Biology-driven AI
生物驱动人工智能
AI Diagnostics
AI诊断

Utilizing multi-modal AI to turn complex biological data into actionable longevity insights. We endorse companies applying machine learning to multi-omics, imaging, and clinical data for aging-specific applications.

利用多模态人工智能，将复杂的生物数据转化为可行的长寿洞察。我们支持将机器学习应用于多组学、影像和临床数据的企业。

💊
Healthcare SaaS / Insurtech
医疗 SaaS / 保险科技
Digital Health Platforms
数字健康平台

Building scalable, data-driven platforms that align financial incentives with proactive health outcomes. We support companies designing the infrastructure for preventive care financing across APAC.

构建可扩展的数据驱动平台，使财务激励与主动健康成果相一致。我们支持在亚太地区设计预防性护理融资基础设施的企业。
